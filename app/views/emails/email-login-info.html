What can I use my NHS login for

NHS login lets you access multiple digital health and social care services with a simple, one-time sign-up process.

The benefits of NHS login

NHS login is secure and can be used repeatedly without you having to prove who you are each time.

How to manage your information

You can change your password, email address or phone number in your account management section once you've logged in.

The NHS login button

You can use your NHS login wherever you see the NHS login button. If a website or app shows this button, simply click it and enter your NHS login information for access.

Visit the NHS login information page for more information.
