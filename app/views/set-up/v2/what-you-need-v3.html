Create an NHS login

To create an NHS login you will need around 20 minutes of your time and a phone with signal, as we will text you a security code. This may take longer if you are using assistive technologies.

Prove who you are

You also need to prove who you are using either your photo I.D. or registration details from your GP surgery’s online services.

Option 1: Photo I.D.

Accepted types of photo ID are:

  • Passport
  • UK driving licence
  • European driving licence
  • European national identity card

Option 2: Registration details

Registration details from your GP surgery’s online services include:

  • Linkage key (could be called a Passphrase)
  • O.D.S. Code (could be called an Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
  • Acccount I.D.
